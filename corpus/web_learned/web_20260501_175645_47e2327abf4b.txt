### 3. Executive Order on AI

An initial Executive Order on AI was issued during the Biden administration, Executive Order 14110. In 2025, a new order was introduced, Executive Order 14179, ‘Removing Barriers to American Leadership in Artificial Intelligence.’

This national initiative guides how federal agencies oversee the use of AI in civil rights, national security, and public services.
